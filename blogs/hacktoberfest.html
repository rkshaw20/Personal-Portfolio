What is Hacktoberfest?

Hacktoberfest is the World biggest Open Source event that encourages newbies to participate and create their 1st meaningful pull request in the world of Open Source.It is a month-long event where contibutors are awarded with goodies for contributing to Open Source projects.Last year it was presented by DigitalOcean, appWrite, intel, and deepSource.

When is Hacktoberfest event?

It happens every year in the month of october.

When did Hacktoberfest start?

Hacktoberfest was launched in 2013 with 700 participants. In 2021 it 141,802 participants contributing to the open source projects and making it the world biggiest Open Source event.

Why participate in Hacktoberfest?

The most important reason is that you will learn about the Open Source world, you will learn how to work with a team, how to contribute to a public repositary and apply your skills to a live public projects. After after successfull submission of 4 pull requests you will earn T-shirt and a swag pack and who does not like swag kit?

Who can participate in Hacktoberfest?

Anyone who has aGitHubaccount can register forHacktoberfestwith their GitHub account.

How you can get a Hacktoberfest T-Shirt?

After registering for the event you need to make a 4 valid pull request to a public repositary before the end date of the event.

Does Hacktoberfestis a beginners friendly?

Ofcourse! It is a beginner friendly. Search for a Good First Issue tag in the repositary and read the readme file.If the Issue can be fixed with your skills then start contributing to it.Trust me you will get huge confidence after successfull merging of your pull request.

Some of the stats of Hacktoberfest 2021

  • 194 Countries joined the event.
